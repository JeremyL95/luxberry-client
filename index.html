    About Us

    Luxberry was established in Malaysia by a group of corporate consultants and trainers with the purpose of providing professional advise and solution to organizations.

    Luxberry offers corporate training and consultancy services, including soft skill training, practical training, ISO training, certification programs (e.g., First Aid, Industry 4.0), team building, event management, corporate root cause analysis, business development consultation services, E-commerce consultation, and most of the services are eligible for HRDCorp claims.

    Moreover, we receive support from our parent company, Luxfruit Trading, which is an importer of premium gifts. With close connections to numerous companies, we are able to provide top-notch services for door gifts, event uniforms, event venues, logistics, and any training-related goods/services.
